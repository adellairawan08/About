SETH System

SETH is a portal website and app that records covid testing data of a person.

SETH is an innovation that supports saver traveling during this pandemic era. We divide our clients into 3 groups, that is group A consists of the test provider such as hospitals and public health centers. Group B consists of places that require the covid testing certificate like airports, train stations, and hotels.
